NastyThermostat

All parts are designed in Fusion 360 and available as STL, STEP and F3D. Print in PETG for best results — PLA also works as an alternative.

Material PETG (PLA also works)
Infill 20–30%
Layer height 0.2mm
Supports Not required
Design tool Fusion 360

Part 1 of 2

GT2 Pulley 138T

The large gear ring that fits around the Nest thermostat dial. Attaches to the metal Nest ring with double-sided tape at 3 points. The GT2 belt runs along the outer teeth to connect with the stepper motor pulley.

⬇ STL ⬇ STEP ⬇ F3D (Fusion 360)

Part 2 of 2

Mounting Plate & Stand

The main frame that holds the Nest thermostat, stepper motor, electronics and all components. The stand clips into the bottom of the mounting plate and keeps the assembly upright on a surface. Both parts are included in the Fusion 360 and STEP files; individual STL files are available below for direct slicing.

⬇ STEP (complete) ⬇ F3D (Fusion 360)
